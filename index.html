Molly est ici!

This is my slice of life in Paris — a creative mix of journal entries, adventures, photography, videos, and random ideas. I am making this as a scrapbook/journal for my time in Paris. 2 months is a long time in a new city, and I hope this provides you with a snapshot of my life (the good and the bad) here. Follow along for fun ✌️

Currently (Updated: July 25, 2025)

It is my last week in Paris as I am writing this, and it has been such a wonderful experience. I definitely fell in love with this city while being here for 2 months. I desperately hope to come back and live here again. If anything, this program and experience has taught me that I am capable of living away from home and that the world has so much to offer. I am going to miss my friends who don't go to Cal but I will forever cherish the time I've had here.
